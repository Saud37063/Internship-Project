AquaGuard AI Advisory & Usage System
🌊 Overview

AquaGuard AI Advisory & Usage System is an AI-powered sustainability project developed as part of a Virtual AI Sustainability Internship. The system is designed to promote responsible water usage, provide intelligent water-related recommendations, and support sustainable water resource management through data-driven insights.

The project leverages Artificial Intelligence to analyze water usage patterns, generate advisory recommendations, and encourage water conservation practices aligned with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal (SDG) 6: Clean Water and Sanitation.

🎯 Project Objectives
Monitor and analyze water usage data.
Provide AI-driven recommendations for efficient water consumption.
Promote awareness of water conservation practices.
Support sustainable management of water resources.
Contribute towards achieving SDG 6 (Clean Water and Sanitation).
